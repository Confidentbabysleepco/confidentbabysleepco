Aria gained a whole pound! 🎉

Weighed June 19 — one pound gained. That is an incredible result and a reflection of every single feed, every pump, every night you showed up. You should feel so proud. 💛

About the cluster feeding: Cluster feeding — where Aria feeds very frequently and barely makes it 3 hours between feeds — is completely normal at 3 weeks and actually a really positive sign. It is her way of going through a growth spurt and signalling your body to increase supply to match her needs. It is exhausting but it is not a sign that something is wrong or that you don't have enough milk. It will settle. 💛
