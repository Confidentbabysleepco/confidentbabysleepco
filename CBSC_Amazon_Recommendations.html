First Night Must Haves 🌙

Before anything else — make sure you have these ready for the first night home.

Muslin cloths — have them everywhere
Feeding supplies — breast or bottle, both ready
Diapers & wipes — always within reach
Easy on/off onesies or pjs — baby does not need cute uncomfortable outfits tonight
Swaddle — the right one makes all the difference
White noise machine — on all night
Night light — dim enough to not wake baby
Nursing chair — your comfort matters too
